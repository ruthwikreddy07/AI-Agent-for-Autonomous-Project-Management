Core Capabilities

Enterprise-Grade AI Features

Thirteen specialized LangChain tools working in concert to deliver autonomous project orchestration, sprint management, and predictive risk intelligence.

Intelligent Planning

Decomposes goals into ordered tasks with topological sorting, smart timelines that skip weekends/holidays, and per-task budget estimation.

execute_project_plan

Self-Healing Schedules

Detects overdue tasks, reschedules to next business day, and cascades changes through entire dependency chains.

heal_project_schedule

Skill-Based Assignment

Scans MongoDB roster, matches task keywords to skills and roles, resolves Trello member IDs automatically.

auto_assign_owner

RAG Project Memory

Upload documents, chunk and embed via Gemini, store in Pinecone for context-aware AI responses.

consult_project_memory

Calendar Management

Two-step availability check + booking flow with Google Meet links, focus time blocks, and smart slot discovery.

schedule_meeting_tool

Human-in-the-Loop

All plans require explicit manager approval before execution. Full transparency with cost breakdowns and timelines.

approve / reject

Sprint Planning Engine

AI groups tasks into time-boxed sprints with capacity budgets, burndown tracking, and scope overload detection.

auto_plan_sprint

Gantt Chart Timeline

Canvas-rendered Gantt chart with dependency arrows, critical path highlighting, today marker, zoom controls, and epic filtering.

gantt-data

AI Risk Prediction

Pre-mortem analysis with 5×5 probability × impact matrix, ranked risk register, and AI-generated mitigation strategies.

predict_risks

Scope Creep Detector

Monitors sprint capacity in real-time. Flags overloaded sprints (>110%) and suggests specific tasks to defer.

detect_scope_creep

Time Tracking

Log actual hours per task, compare against estimates, and feed real data into sprint burndown charts.

log_time

RBAC Access Control

Three-tier role system (Admin, PM, Developer) protecting 14 sensitive endpoints with FastAPI middleware.

require_role
